    • Van die Rekord af

    Dit is my persoonlike gedagtes, nie om aangehaal te word nie!

    Ek is gebore, en toe ja, ek het visioene van my hele lewe gehad. Ek moes eers kruip voor ek kon loop en die slegte dinge stop.

    Hoekom kan ek nie kommunikeer nie?

    Toe ek begin loop, het ek besef dat kommunikasie en grootword tyd vat, en die lewe tik verby sekonde vir sekonde. Ek sien daar is dinge wat ek nie sal kan stop nie omdat ek nog besig is om groot te word, en dit is moeilik om dinge van die toekoms aan mense te verduidelik. Ek sal net moet probeer om die groot dinge te stop of 'n manier vind om die slegte te keer.

    Wag, iets is nie reg met my brein nie. In my toekoms sien ek geestesprobleme.

    Ek moet vir iemand vertel!

    Maar hoekom word mense met geestesprobleme so sleg behandel? As ek iemand vertel, gaan hulle my toesluit.

    Nou is daar 'n groot probleem!

    Ek kan nie die slegte stop as ek nie vir mense sê nie, maar as ek dit doen, en hulle sluit my toe, wie gaan dan help?

    Okay, dit is vreemd. Ek kry speelgoed, daar is bene om my, en baie geraas. Dit gaan in die toekoms gebeur. Ek onthou 'n flits van wanneer ek loop en saam met ander speel. Sal enigiemand iets doen oor die slegte dinge?

    Nou is ek 3 jaar oud, en ander kinders is vreemd! Dit is asof hulle geen idee het van toekomstige gebeure nie. Miskien hoef ek niks te doen nie. Ek sal dit net vergeet. Dit is wat ek sal doen. As daar iemand anders soos ek is, kan ons mense soos ons bymekaarkry en die toekoms vir goed verander.

    Daar is iemand met wie ek kan praat, maar Hy praat nie direk terug nie. Daar is 'n Boek, en dit wys waar ons vandaan kom. Ek onthou Hy, of Hulle, is belangrik in my lewe.

    Hulle stuur pendeltuie die ruimte in. Beteken dit hulle weet van die dinge wat uit die lug gaan val in die toekoms en baie mense doodmaak?

    Een pendeltuig gaan ontplof. Hulle het gesê as iets verkeerd gaan, sal hulle die lansering kanselleer. Ek moet dit stop, maar ek is nie seker of hulle dit gaan lanseer nie, aangesien hulle gesê het alles is perfek voor die lansering.

    Die pendeltuig het ontplof. Hoekom het dit gebeur? Ek het die polisie gebel om te vra, maar my suster het gesê ek moet nie met die telefoon speel as my ouers nie by die huis is nie. Ek moet wag om hulle te vra.

    Ek gaan binnekort vyf word. Ek gaan my ma vra waar hierdie plek is waar vliegtuie gaan invlieg. En dan... My suster het 'n boek; ek gaan haar vra om dinge te verduidelik sodat ek dit kan teken en my ma vra. My suster wil nie hê ek moet in haar boek teken nie, maar 'n meisie by die crèche het vir my een gegee. Ons is in die motor, en dit is 'n goeie tyd om te vra. Ek het 'n paar figure geteken, maar ek is nog nie goed hierin nie. Ek sal my ma vra.

    Waar is daar hoë geboue naby? … Hoekom sou iemand vliegtuie in hoë geboue wil laat invlieg? …

    Niemand verstaan wat ek sê nie. Ons is in oorlog, en daar is bom-oefeninge, brand-oefeninge, en aardbewing-oefeninge. Ek verstaan nie. Hoekom weet ek hierdie dinge gaan nie nou gebeur nie, maar ander is so bang vir die toekoms?

    My ma het gesê ek moet ophou praat oor hierdie dinge, want dit is ernstige dinge en ek kan in die moeilikheid kom.

    Die Boek sê jy moet jou ouers gehoorsaam, so ek gaan hierdie toekomstige visioene en probleme toesluit totdat ek ouer is en iets daaraan kan doen.

    Ek kry déjà vu's, en mense sê hulle kry dit ook, maar soos die ou mense sê, dit gebeur net een of twee keer in 'n leeftyd. Hoekom kry ek permanente déjà vu, met kennis van die toekoms?

    Om jou lewe te prioritiseer is goed, sê die gerespekteerde ouderlinge. Ek gaan nou nie optree nie.

    Hoekom weet ek hierdie dinge? Hoekom voel dit asof iemand anders wat nou leef my gedagtes lees en my beskerm teen mense wat my kan mislei of my gawe vir hul eie slegte doeleindes wil gebruik?

    Wel, ek is nou agt-en-dertig en moet vir mense sê dat ek herinneringe aan die toekoms gehad het.

    In 2025, in die derde kwartaal, sal oorlog wêreldwyd eskaleer. Alle tegnologieë gaan in gevaar wees om verlore te gaan as gevolg van hierdie oorlog. Dit is 'n oorlog van hoë infrastruktuurvernietiging en lae sterftesyfers. Dit beteken mense gaan óf in neutrale lande met min infrastruktuur leef, óf die res van die wêreld gaan sonder die geriewe leef wat ons as vanselfsprekend aanvaar. Die oorlog begin aan die einde van die derde kwartaal van 2024.

    Teen die tweede kwartaal van 2025 sal dit in volle gang wees. In 2026 sal geïsoleerde sakke van oorlog voorkom, met KI wat betrokke raak. Teen 2027 sal burgeroorloë lande oorheers.

    En in Maart, Augustus en September van 2026 of 2028 sal 'n voorwerp uit die ruimte Nieu-Seeland tref. (Uit my notas.)

    As ek klippe wat die aarde gaan tref aanmeld sonder bewys, gaan niemand my glo totdat dit te laat is en ek duideliker besonderhede kan verskaf nie.

    Ruimteagentskappe het 'n groot lugruim om dop te hou en te soek na hierdie dodelike voorwerpe. As ek hulle waarsku, kan ek hulle dalk aflei van die een wat hulle op hul eie sou gevind het. Om my visioene bo hul werk te prioritiseer is dalk nie 'n goeie idee nie.

    Dit het gelei tot die stigting van Mensdom Oorlewing.

    Die waarheid is, ek het nie 'n perfekte plan om ruimtevaarders wat in die moeilikheid is te red of om asteroïdes te keer nie.

    Ek is skisofrenies en op medikasie wat my gedagtes so stadig maak dat dit jare neem om enigiets te bereik.

    Ek het ernstige hulp nodig. Ware helde met 'n passie om honderde duisende lewens te red van asteroïdes of rotse wat die aarde gaan tref.

    Elke mens moet verstaan dat ons almal op hierdie planeet is, en ons moet saamwerk om die aarde en onsself te red.

    Een ding wat ek besef het: Vra vir my die regte vraag, en inligting wat ek nie geweet het ek het nie, sal na vore kom, en jy mag net jou antwoord kry.

    Het mense wat die toekoms onthou die mag om dit te verander? Sal hulle weet as hulle dit verander het? Het hul pogings om dit te verander die toekoms laat waar word, of verander dit vanself? Kan 'n gewone mens die verloop van wat gaan gebeur verander? Sal enigiemand weet as die toekoms verander is? Laat die Skepper mense toe om die toekoms te sien om dit te verander, of om voor te berei daarvoor? Wat is die doel om die toekoms te ken?

    Off the Record

    These are my personal thoughts, not to be quoted!

    I was born, and then yes, I had visions of my whole life. I had to crawl before I could walk and stop the bad things. Why can't I communicate? When I started walking, I realized that communication and growing up take time, and life ticks by second by second. I see there are things I won't be able to stop because I'm still growing, and it's hard to explain things about the future to people. I'll just have to try to stop the big things or find a way to prevent the bad. Wait, something isn't right with my brain. In my future, I see mental problems. I should tell someone!

    But why are people with mental issues treated so badly? If I tell someone, they'll lock me up. Now there's a big problem! I can't stop the bad if I don't tell people, but if I do, and they lock me up, who will help then?

    Okay, this is strange. I'm getting toys, there are legs around me, and lots of noise. This is going to happen in the future. I remember a flash of when I walk and play with others. Will anyone do something about the bad things?

    Now I'm 3 years old, and other kids are strange! It's like they have no idea about future events. Maybe I don't need to do anything. I'll just forget it. That's what I'll do. If there is someone else like me, we could gather people like us and change the future for good.

    There's someone I can talk to, but He doesn't speak back directly. There's a Book, and it shows where we came from. I remember He, or They, are important in my life.

    They're sending shuttles into space. Does that mean they know about the things that will fall from the sky in the future and cause many deaths?

    One shuttle is going to explode. They said if something goes wrong, they'll abort the launch. I should stop it, but I'm not sure if they'll launch it, as they've said everything is perfect before the launch.

    The shuttle exploded. Why did this happen? I called the police to ask, but my sister said not to play with the phone when my parents weren't home. I'll have to wait to ask them.

    I'll be five soon. I'm going to ask my mom where this place is where planes are going to fly into. And then… My sister has a book; I'll ask her to explain things so I can draw them and ask my mom. My sister doesn't want me to draw in her book, but a girl at daycare gave me one. We're in the car, and this is a good time to ask. I've drawn some figures, but I'm not good at this yet. I'll ask my mom.

    Where are there tall buildings nearby? … Why would someone want to fly planes into tall buildings? …

    No one understands what I'm saying. We're at war, and there are bomb drills, fire drills, and earthquake drills. I don't understand. Why do I know these things won't happen now, but others are so scared of the future?

    My mom said to stop talking about these things because it's serious, and I could get into trouble.

    The Book says to obey your parents, so I'm going to lock these future visions and problems away until I'm older and can do something about them.

    I'm getting déjà vu's, and people say they get déjà vu too, but as the older people say, it only happens once or twice in a lifetime. Why do I get permanent déjà vu, knowing things about the future?

    Prioritizing your life is good, or so the respected elders say. I'm not going to act on this now.

    Why do I know these things? Why does it feel like someone else alive now is reading my thoughts and protecting me from people who might mislead or use me and my gift for their own bad purposes?

    Well, I'm thirty-eight now, and I need to tell people that I've had memories of the future. In 2025, in the third quarter, war will escalate worldwide. All technologies will be at risk of being lost due to this war. It's a war of high infrastructure destruction and low casualties. This means people will either live in neutral countries with little infrastructure, or the rest of the world will live without the comforts we take for granted. The war starts by the end of the third quarter of 2024. By the second quarter of 2025, it will be in full swing. In 2026, isolated pockets of war will occur, with AI joining in. By 2027, civil wars will take over countries. And in March, August, and September of 2026 or 2028, an object from space will hit New Zealand. (From my notes.)

    If I report rocks heading to Earth without proof, no one will believe me until it's too late and I can provide clearer details.

    Space agencies have a vast sky to watch, searching for these deadly objects. If I alert them, I might distract them from finding one they would have discovered on their own. Prioritizing my visions over their work might not be a good idea.

    Thus, the start-up of Humanity Survival.

    The truth is, I don't have a perfect plan to save astronauts in trouble or prevent asteroids from hitting Earth.

    I'm schizophrenic, and my medication slows my mind, making it take years to accomplish anything.

    I need serious help. True heroes with a passion for saving hundreds of thousands of lives from asteroids or rocks that will hit Earth.

    Everyone must understand that we are all on this planet together, and we must work together to save Earth and ourselves.

    One thing I've realized: Ask me the right question, and information I didn't know I had will surface, and you might just get your answer.

    Do people who remember the future have the power to change it? Will they know if they've changed it? Do their attempts to change it make the future come true, or does the future change on its own? Can an ordinary human alter the course of what's to come? Will anyone know if the future was changed? Does the Creator allow people to see the future so they can change it or prepare for it? What is the purpose of knowing the future?

    On 22 November, 2029, a significant asteroid is predicted to impact Earth, with the estimated point of collision being near New Zealand.
