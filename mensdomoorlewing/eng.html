    For Humanity, our Planet, and lost space travelers!

    Mensdom Oorlewing is dedicated to saving lives in space and protecting Earth.

    Our mission is to bring together a dynamic team of young thinkers to prevent collisions with cosmic objects and support space travelers in need.

    Mensdom Oorlewing remains independent from other space agencies, with a focus on the challenges of space safety. We will adhere to international standards while encouraging volunteers of all ages and fields worldwide to get involved. An updated website will soon be available for registration and participation in our mission.

    An asteroid, comet, meteorite - call it what you will - is potentially on a course to wipe out life on Earth.

    Maybe not in your lifetime or even in that of your fifth-generation descendants. Yet, there's always the possibility that such an event could happen tomorrow.
